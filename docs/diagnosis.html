AI Diagnosis Engine

Interactive Rare Disease Diagnosis

Enter patient symptoms in natural language. Our AI engine extracts HPO terms, cross-references the knowledge graph, and generates differential diagnoses with confidence scores.

Workflow

4-Step Diagnostic Process

1. Symptom Input

Describe symptoms in natural language or structured HPO terms

2. HPO Extraction

AI extracts standardized Human Phenotype Ontology terms

3. Knowledge Match

Cross-reference against 2847 disease-gene-phenotype associations

4. Consensus Dx

10 AI agents debate and reach consensus with confidence scores
