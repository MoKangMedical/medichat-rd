Drug Repurposing

AI-driven identification of existing drugs that may treat rare diseases, reducing development time from 15 years to 3-5 years.

Assessment Dimensions

How Drug Repurposing Works

Target Overlap

Analyze intersection between drug targets and disease-associated genes using OpenTargets data.

Literature Evidence

PubMed literature retrieval with evidence polarity classification (supporting/opposing/neutral).

Genomic Data

OpenTargets association scoring with multi-dimensional evidence from GWAS, ClinVar, and pathway data.

Quality Gate

Data completeness, evidence consistency, and reproducibility checks for each assessment.

AI Scoring

Comprehensive scoring algorithm with recommendation levels and confidence assessment.

Clinical Trials

Cross-reference with active clinical trials to identify real-world repurposing opportunities.
