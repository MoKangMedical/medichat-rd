FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a free trial?

Yes. All plans include a 14-day free trial with full feature access. No credit card required to start.

Can I change plans later?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect at the start of the next billing cycle.

Is MediChat-RD HIPAA compliant?

The Enterprise plan includes HIPAA BAA and full compliance documentation. All data is encrypted with AES-256 at rest and TLS 1.3 in transit.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, Amex), bank transfers, and purchase orders for Enterprise plans.

Do you offer academic discounts?

Yes. We offer 40% discounts for academic institutions and research organizations. Contact sales with your .edu email for verification.
