FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What rare diseases does MediChat-RD cover?

MediChat-RD covers 7,000+ rare diseases across 9 categories: neurological, hematological, metabolic, connective tissue, autoimmune, dermatological, ophthalmological, pulmonary, and genetic disorders. Our database is sourced from OMIM, Orphanet, and ClinVar.

How accurate is the AI diagnosis?

Our multi-agent system achieves 94.7% diagnostic accuracy in benchmark testing across 500+ validated rare disease cases. The system provides confidence scores and recommends confirmatory tests for each diagnosis.

Is patient data secure?

Yes. MediChat-RD is HIPAA and GDPR compliant. All data is encrypted with AES-256 at rest and TLS 1.3 in transit. We maintain complete audit logs and support on-premise deployment for enterprise customers.
